What is Meal Hippo?

Meal Hippo connects you to chefs and caterers in your area, who offer home-style cooking that you can enjoy at home.

How does Meal Hippo work?

Step 1—Build your order online

Choose from several home-style dishes offered by chefs in your area.

Explore dishes

Step 2—Checkout

Set the date and time that you would like to receive your order.

Choose the location that you would like your order delivered to you.

Finally, pay online using our secure payment service.

Step 3—Receive your order

Your fresh, ready-to-eat food is delivered to you, as part of every order.

We make sure that you get your food safely.

Option—Subscriptions

When you subscribe, you can set up weekly orders for a day of the week and time of the day of your choosing.

You can choose a different dish every week based on what's available.

Save 5% on every order when you subscribe.

Cancel your subscription anytime.

You can subscribe after logging in by clicking on the subscription toggle button.

Guarantee

If you're not satisfied, just let us know and we'll give you your money back.

How big are the portions?

Portion sizes follow the amounts suggested by Dietitions of Canada's servings guide, based on the Canada Food Guide.

Where do the dishes come from?

All food is prepared by professional, independent chefs in your area.

Where does the nutritional information come from?

The nutritional information (calories, protein, fat, and carbs) is an estimated range based on Health Canada's Nutrient Data and other sources.

Other details

All currency is in Canadian Dollars.

All your data is kept private. We never share it with anyone.

All of your payments are processed securely using Stripe.
