What is Meal Hippo?

Meal Hippo is a web application that connects you to chefs and caterers in your area, who offer home-style cooking that you can enjoy at home.

How does Meal Hippo work?

Step 1—Build your order online

Choose from several home-style dishes offered by chefs in your area.

Specify whether you would like your order delivered or if you would like to pick it up.

Explore dishes

Step 2—Checkout

Set the date and time that you would like to receive your order.

If you chose delivery, select the location that you would like your order delivered to you.

Finally, pay online using our secure payment system.

Step 3—Receive your order

Your fresh, ready-to-eat food is delivered to you, or you pick it up at the supplier's location.

We make sure you get your food safely.

Option—Subscription

When you subscribe, you can set up weekly orders for a day of the week and time of the day of your choosing.

In subsequent weeks, we'll email you a reminder before the day and time that you set. You can accept the upcoming order, change the dishes or timing, or skip that week.

You can choose different dishes every week.

Save 5% on every order when you subscribe.

Cancel your subscription anytime.

You can subscribe after logging in by clicking on the subscription toggle button in the navigation menu.

Guarantee

If you're not satisfied, just let us know and we'll give you a full refund.

How big are the portions?

Portion sizes follow the amounts suggested by Dietitions of Canada's servings guide, based on the Canada Food Guide.

Where do the dishes come from?

All food is prepared by professional, independent chefs or caterers in your area.

The chef and cateter name is shown on the dishes page.

Where does the nutritional information come from?

The nutritional information (calories, protein, fat, and carbs) are calculated directly from the ingredients in the chef or caterer's recipe.

How does pricing work?

Chefs and caterers set their own prices for the items they sell. This is the price that's posted on the dishes page. Chefs and caterers receive 100% of their price.

Meal Hippo applies a delivery fee, which covers the cost of delivery. This isn't applied when you choose to pick your order.

Meal Hippo also applies a service fee, which covers Meal Hippo's costs associated with payment transactions, servers, and software development.

Other details

All currency is in Canadian Dollars.

All your data is kept private. We never share it with anyone.

All of your payments are processed securely using Stripe.
